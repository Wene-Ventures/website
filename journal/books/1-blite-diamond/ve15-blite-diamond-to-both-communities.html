for your secret, I have just gained,

from the wife, you caused deep heart pains,

TO MONDALA'S MOTHER
Kilio nimesikia,
na uchungu kuuona,
Hakika zangu hisia,
kwa kamba umezishona,
Nashindwa kuvumilia,
kushindwa kwako kupona,
Kondoo ntakuchinjia,
na kuku aliyenona.

TO DIANA'S AUNT
Don't just teach the word but live it,
show love by pushing for a change,
If God lives your actions should prove it,
if Bible is law don't skip some pages,
You keep getting lost bit by bit,
tainting your faith by birthing rage,
Although you will never admit,
you are too living in a cage.

TO THE BLACK CHOIR
Imani ni uhakika,
wa mambo yenu yajayo,
Maneno kila dakika,
yaliyopo na yajayo,
Endelea kuyashika,
kwa bidii ka' zakayo,
Ipo siku utafika,
kwenye raha ya babayo.

TO DIANA'S UNCLE
I see the look you give sister,
you better leave it in your pants,
Stay away from her black glitter,
and stop giving her your free grants,
To all your guests you cause blisters,
those who don't make it return as plants,
With their hearts turned cold and bitter,
their stomachs with living implants.

TO UMOJA
Dunia ya kakamaa,
na nyuso kukunjamana,
Yacheka tukitambaa,
na tukiinamiana,
Wandugu na waamaa,
msiache 'shikamana,
Msije kata tamaa,
mkaacha kupendana.

TO DIANA'S CLASSMATES
You seriously need to grow up,
my sister is not your play tool,
Some of your words make me throw up,
bullying doesn't make you look cool,
Soon all your hatred will blow up,
and then you will feel like pure fools,
Kindness will make you glow up,
and manners will make you love school.

TO MONDALA'S BROTHERS
Myatunze maumivu,
kwenye moyo na akili,
Msijipake majivu,
ili rangi kubadili,
Chochea uvumilivu,
ukipokea fimbo mbili,

na kulia kusizidi.

Is this the reform you talk about?
I see how you look at my brothers,
I am yet just to call you out,
and report you to my fathers,
What if all your lab rats run out,
will you then move to my mother?

TO MONDALA'S SISTER

zuia kujidharau,

japo umenizidi dau,

and leave me with grandfather,

TO MONDALA'S FATHER

umeshindwa irejesha?

bado hawajakuchosha?

TO DIANA
Yes, you are very beautiful,
but also you are very kind,

not to show love of any kind,
In secret you are prayerful,
but in open you remain blind,
You should openly be cheerful,
I hope your courage you will find.

TO MONDALA

kama baba naye mama,

bila kugeuka nyuma,
Usitamke laana,
hata ukikosa nyama,
Ukiona pamebana,
ujipe moyo wa chuma.
