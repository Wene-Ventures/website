Blite Diamond

Ve0 - Blite Diamond Introduction

At the peak of the slavery era in Britain, a British community bought several enslaved Tanzanians from the slave market to further their community and get help when needed.

In an unexpected twist, the first and only mixed-race child is born, and because of it, things turn upside down for both slaves and masters in this community. Some see her birth as a hope for unity and equality, while others see it as an abomination that needs to be corrected.

Her slave family calls her black, while her free family calls her White. Her mother names her Mondala, while her father names her Diana. Without an identity or known purpose, her name ends up being Blite Diamond since she is both Black & White and Diana & Mondala.

Will she successfully know her purpose and take part in bringing a new change, or will she cause pain to both her families?

After a series of events caused mainly by the birth of Blite Diamond, a significant change is needed to help

You will find Diana, and Mondala used interchangeably depending on who is addressing her. If she is addressed by her white family (The Masters), then she is called Diana, however, she is called Mondala by her black family (The Slaves)

Below are the characters from both families of Blite Diamond. Kindly note that Blite Diamond is given two personalities in this book, Diana and Mondala, who both represent the same person but different ethnicity (personality).

Main Character

  • Diana - White personality of Blite Diamond
  • Mondala - Black personality of Blite Diamond
  • Blite Diamond - Mixed personality of Blite Diamond

Masters (The White Community)

This is a list of all the people related to Blite Diamond from her white side of the family, the masters.

  • Diana's Father - Also known as Paul, he is the leader of the white community (masters) and the father of Blite Diamond. He took advantage of Blite Diamond's bith mother and accidentally got her pregant.
  • Diana's Mom (Stepmom) - Bitter for the love that her husband has for her stepdaughter, she makes sure to punish Blite Diamond's mom for being a mother, while she remains barren.
  • Diana's Aunt - Also known as Vicar, she is the spiritual leader and evangelist of the bunch. She believes in Christianity and has been teaching the Black community to be submissive to their masters. She is consious and unhappy about slavery but knows better than to challenge her community.
  • Diana's Uncle - Also known as Victor. He is the most evil man in the white community. Taking advantage of whoever he likes and makes it a point to make the lives of his slaves miserable.
  • Diana's Classmates - Studying in the same class as Blite Diamond, they can't help but fall in a love hate relationship with the first mixed-race girl in their world.
  • Doctor - Empathetic of the slaves, he is the one responsible for making sure all slaves are well to continue working and to patch them when they are broken. Little does everyone know, he has a little secret of his own.
