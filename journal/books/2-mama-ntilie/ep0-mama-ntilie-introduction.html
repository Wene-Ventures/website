Mama Ntilie is a love story between two people from different spectrums of life. One is a poor woman who has had a very tough upbringing to becoming the woman she is, and the other is a rich politician man who has always had support from his parents.

When they meet in an unexpected way and in an unexepected place at an unexpected time, will their love survive or be shortlived?
